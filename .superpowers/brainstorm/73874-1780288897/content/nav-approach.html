A — Persistent Sidebar

Groups functions into labeled sections (Project / Compliance / Finance). Always visible. No tabs at all — clicking a section item replaces the main panel. Standard in Procore, Aconex, Jira.

C — Group Filters + Focused Tabs

Keep the two-level nav but make group chips first-class (larger, more prominent). Clicking a group shows only that group's tabs — 5-7 visible at once instead of 50+. Upgrade of existing pattern.

Click to select. These can be mixed — e.g. sidebar for role nav + focused tabs within a section.
