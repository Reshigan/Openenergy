Offtaker

Three calm bets for a non-expert buyer: (1) a bill-to-savings guided path, one decision per screen · (2) a consolidated-PPA cockpit that rolls up the whole portfolio · (3) a sustainability ledger that makes REC / Scope-2 provenance the hero. The quietest interface of any role — mobile-first throughout.
The person
Head of Energy & Sustainability at a C&I retail group (e.g. Goldrush). Buys power for dozens of sites; not a trader or lawyer. Lives between a desk and a phone in site visits. Mental model is simple: “my bill is too high → get cleaner, cheaper supply.” Needs plain language, calm pacing, and one obvious next action — never a terminal.
