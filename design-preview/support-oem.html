SUPPORT / OEM field service · service desk

Built for the tech in gloves, not the analyst at a desk.

Thandi is an OEM field-service technician and Tier-2 service-desk owner. Half her week is at remote solar & wind sites — direct sunlight, gloves on, one-bar LTE that drops in the switchyard. The other half she runs the SLA clock on P1–P4 tickets. She needs work orders, warranty/RMA, spare parts and a predictive read on which asset fails next — usable one-handed, offline, glance-fast.

Mobile is the primary surface. Three bets: a glove-friendly field-ops PWA (offline cards, big taps, camera-to-RMA), a SLA-clock dispatch board for desk triage, and a predictive asset-health console that puts RUL / anomaly / fault-fingerprint ahead of reactive NTT-style O&M.
