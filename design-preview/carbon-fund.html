Carbon Fund / Registry

Persona — Project developer & fund manager

Runs a portfolio of REIPPPP / VCM projects from PDD through issuance to retirement. Lives inside registration documents, MRV verifications, Article 6 corresponding-adjustment ledgers and ERPA forward-delivery schedules. Spends the day cross-referencing Verra / Gold Standard registry IDs against the platform's own state.

Environment: dual-monitor desktop, deep document work, long sessions. Phone is for on-the-move attestation — approving a retirement, checking a delivery window, signing a clause — not heavy editing.

Non-negotiable: provenance and a tamper-evident audit trail. Every tonne must be traceable to its PDD, its verification body, its serial range, and its corresponding adjustment. Double-counting ends a fund.

Why these three directions

The carbon role is fundamentally about chain of custody over a unit (tCO₂e). So one direction makes the credit's life-story the literal UI (Ledger); one treats the platform as a certifying registry where the certified export is the hero (Workspace); and one serves the market-maker instinct — spot/forward OTC book with quality ratings (Trading Book). Same forest-provenance identity, three jobs.
