The Fleet Floor

Runs O&M across a fleet of commercial & industrial solar sites (the Goldrush portfolio, live on Solax telemetry). Half the day is in the office watching uptime and guarantee economics; half is in the field with a phone, a permit, and a torque wrench. Lives in work orders, PM compliance, permit-to-work/LOTO, asset prognostics, availability guarantees & liquidated damages, PR underperformance and spare parts.

Three bets for one split persona: a spatial fleet brain for the dispatcher who thinks in geography, a guarantee/LD cockpit for the contract owner who thinks in money-at-risk, and an O&M work loop for the operator who lives in the daily PM→WO→permit grind. Telemetry-driven, actuals-only — every number traces to a real inverter poll, never a synthetic kWh.
