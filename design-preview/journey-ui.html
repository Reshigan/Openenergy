What changes, in one place

  • Menu is the work. The rail lists outcomes a role owns, capped at five plus Today. Reference views move inside the journey that needs them.
  • The journey is visible. Each chain's real states become a lane; live cases sit at the stage they are in, so progress and pressure read at a glance.
  • Create where you work. The primary create sits on the journey; advancing a case happens on the case. No separate builder, no +New picker.
  • One case, in place. Opening a case expands it into its lifecycle with the next legitimate actions and their downstream effects. Thread folds in.
  • Onboarding is one outcome. Three steps to a real first record and a non-empty board, seeded with the role's own primary entity.
  • Nothing is orphaned. Every function maps to exactly one journey, so the flat catalogue can retire without losing a tool.
