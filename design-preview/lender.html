Lenderrole: lender · project finance

Who: A project-finance credit officer at a DFI or commercial bank — books R200m–R3bn senior debt against REIPPPP / private-wire IPPs. Lives in covenants, DSCR/LLCR, reserve accounts (DSRA/MRA), IE certifications, security perfection, IFRS9 staging and Equator Principles. Environment: dual-monitor desk, deep concentration, audit-trail obsessed, decisions go to credit committee. Device: desktop for analysis and certified exports; phone for time-boxed approvals and watch-list alerts on the move.

Why these directions: the lender's value is judgment under evidence — so all three are sober, data-dense and tamper-evident. They differ in what the day organises around: portfolio risk (console), the facility-as-file (dossier), or the queue of gated decisions (command center).
