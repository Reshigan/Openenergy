Persona — who's holding the tablet

Site construction project manager on a utility-scale solar/wind EPC. Splits the day between the site — hard-hat, gloves, glare, patchy LTE, walking witness/hold inspections with the engineer and subcontractors, and the trailer/office desktop running document control. Lives inside submittals, ITPs, NCRs and the punch list; measured on whether the plant reaches COD with a clean handover dossier. Stress is concrete: open NCRs stall trades, a missed hold point reworks a whole array, and document control decides who gets paid.

Why these three directions

A site PM doesn't browse — they need "what blocks me today" answered in one glance, then the ability to act in the field on a tablet. So the three bets diverge on where the work lives: a tablet day-board for the walk, a document-control register for the desk, and a quality / ITP inspection tracker for witness & hold discipline.

Overarching theme · EASE

For a site PM, ease = today's inspections and open NCRs surface on a tablet-ready board, act on site. Every option below makes “what blocks progress today” obvious, then one-thumb actionable in the field.
