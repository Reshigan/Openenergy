Regulator Workbench

Thandeka Mahlangu — Regulatory Officer, NERSA Electricity Regulation Branch. Desktop-bound, deliberate, evidence-first. Spends the day working a cross-chain case inbox: licence adjudications, on-site compliance inspections, s35 enforcement, dispositions and certified export packs. Every decision must be defensible on the record and survive appeal. Mobile is for inspection days in the field — capture findings, check an SLA clock, never lose the audit trail. Stress lives in three places: SLA breach, defensibility, and the audit chain.
Rationale. A regulator does not browse — they triage and adjudicate against statutory clocks. So the three directions are different bets on the same job: (1) the queue triaged for you by SLA risk, (2) the evidence assembled for you in one certified case file, (3) the enforcement pipeline made visible end-to-end. Every option foregrounds EASE — collapse "where do I start / where's the proof" to near zero.
