Platform Admin Operations & Compliance Control CEC Open Energy Platform · admin@openenergy.co.za

Persona

The platform operator-of-record. Sits at a multi-monitor desk, control-panel mindset, all day in the back office. Underwrites every other role's L5 evidence — when the regulator asks "prove it", the proof comes from here. Lives in fear of three words: tenant isolation breach, tamper-evidence break, platform down. Needs the whole platform's health and audit integrity answerable in one glance, on one screen.

Design rationale

Three distinct bets for a high-trust operator: a command center (health-first), a lifecycle board (tenant flow-first), and an integrity ledger (tamper-evidence-first). Dark, dense, mono-numeric — built for sustained focus, not delight. Every option answers one question instantly: is everything healthy and provably intact?

Option B

Tenant Lifecycle Board

A kanban-of-record for the platform's tenants: every org as a card moving onboard → KYC → active → suspend, with the KYC chain (W198) embedded in-lane. The bet: an operator's real day is moving organisations through states without ever breaking isolation between them.

Ease

Makes effortless: every tenant's exact lifecycle state and isolation status, one board, drag to advance. No tenant is ever in an unknown state, and the isolation outline tells you each one is sealed off.

Option C

The proof-of-record view. A vertical, append-only ledger of sealed audit blocks (W118), certified exports (W119) and control-environment audits (W121), each with its Merkle seal, quorum status and one-click verify. The bet: the admin's deepest job is being able to say "here is the proof, and here is that it is intact" — instantly, to a regulator.

Ease

Makes effortless: tamper-evidence verifiable at a glance — every seal green, every export countersigned, re-verify in one click. The whole chain of custody for every other role's L5 evidence, on one ledger.
