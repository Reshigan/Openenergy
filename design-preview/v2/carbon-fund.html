Design Rationale

Three different bets on the same anxiety — can I prove this credit is real and uniquely retired? Each option foregrounds EASE: the evidence chain is always visible, and a certified export is one click away with proof pre-attached. No purple gradients — archival paper, registry green, a certified-seal gold. It should feel like a registry, not a SaaS dashboard.
