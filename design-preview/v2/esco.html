Asset Performance & O&M Command

Persona

An asset-performance & O&M manager running a distributed PV + BESS fleet across SA sites. Works desktop in the NOC and tablet on-site — gloves, glare, intermittent signal. Lives under availability-LD exposure: every hour of downtime is a contractual penalty. The one question that never stops: which asset needs me next, and why?

Design rationale

O&M is triage, not browsing. Three different bets on how to collapse a noisy fleet into one ranked "act here next" — a predictive asset-health map, an availability/LD cockpit, and a work-order command board. The predictive RUL layer is the differentiator and is foregrounded in every view.

Overarching theme

EASE — the fleet tells you who needs you

For an O&M lead, ease isn't fewer clicks — it's never hunting. The platform ranks the fleet by predicted revenue-at-risk, names the why, and offers one tap to act. Noise in, a single decision out.
