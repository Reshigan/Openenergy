Platform Admin — the exchange operator's console

Three ground-up directions for the operator who runs the exchange itself. Observability + control, with the journey meter made a first-class citizen — because the platform charges per journey, this is where that meter is configured and watched.

◐ Persona

Platform operator running the exchange itself. Owns tenants, users & roles, the seven cron schedules, the revenue & fee engine, market halt/resume, system health, Merkle audit-chain integrity and surveillance. Lives on a wide desktop with a NOC mindset and pager-duty reflexes — calm until something breaks, then fast. On mobile they don't browse; they respond to incidents: halt the market, ack an alert, drain a DLQ.

desktop-primarymulti-monitor mobile = incident-responselow-light NOC

◑ Why these 3 directions

The operator carries two heads at once — SRE (is the system healthy?) and CFO (is the meter earning?). One layout can't serve both, so each direction commits to a different bet: a live observability cockpit, a revenue & journey-metering console, and a tenant/control console with the market big-red-button. Near-black NOC palette, monospace telemetry, signal-coded states.
