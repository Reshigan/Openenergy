# Consolidated Energy Cockpit (CEC) — 15-minute product film

**Audience:** United Nations + Global ESCO Network
**Duration:** 15:00 (target)
**Voice:** en-US-AvaMultilingualNeural (Microsoft Edge TTS — conversational, expressive, friendly)
**Pace:** ~150 wpm conversational (rate +6% to land 15:00 at ~2,110 spoken words)
**Word budget:** ~2,110 spoken words
**Visual:** SPA captures at 1920×1080, 30 fps, headed Chromium via Playwright
**Composite:** ffmpeg — V/O over screen + lower-third role chyron, navy/emerald/gold lower-thirds

---

## Editorial principle

This is a **product + value** film. Not a technical tour.

- We say what a person can *do* — and what they *get back*.
- We avoid platform plumbing: no acronyms (no MFA, no SCADA, no Ed25519, no
  Merkle, no covenant arithmetic), no debt-service ratios, no audit-chain
  cryptography, no protocol names.
- Where regulators or technologies must be named, we use plain English
  ("the national energy regulator", "secure sign-in", "a verified history
  of every action").
- Every beat should answer: "If I were this person, why would I want this?"
- The system is **built, deployed, and running today** at oe.vantax.co.za.
  Use present-tense, ready-state language. Never say "we will" when "we do"
  is true. Never call this a pilot, a prototype, a proof-of-concept, or a
  roadmap. The platform is in production.

## Production conventions

- Timing markers `[mm:ss]` are absolute from t=0.
- Screen cues `[SCREEN: <spec-name>]` map 1:1 to a `tests/video/<role>.spec.ts` shot key.
- Lower-third chyrons `[CHYRON: title // subtitle]` appear over the corresponding shot.
- Narrator pauses are written as ellipses `…` (~250ms each); paragraph breaks are ~500ms.
- All on-screen data is the anonymized seed from migration 079 (Phase 1).
